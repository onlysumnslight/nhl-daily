Welcome back Sprong

Nov 8th, 2024

The Seattle Kraken, who, in my opinion, are desperate for goal scoring make a move early in the season to reacquire Daniel Sprong from the Vancouver Canucks.
Sprong, who was on the team in 2022-2023, had his best season with 46 points in 66 games looks to tap back into that form and bring life into this Kraken team.
